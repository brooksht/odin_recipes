Spaghetti and Meatballs

A steaming hot plate of Spaghetti and Meatballs!

A quick and easy entree for any weeknight dinner with the family is spaghetti and meatballs. With a high quality sauce, this simple dish can be elevated to perfection!

It's a super simple recipe which makes it perfect for any occasion, be it a weekday dinner or a supper club? Try this one today, I don't think you'll be disapointed!

You'll need the following ingredients...

  • Tomato Sauce

  • Meatballs

  • Olive Oil

  • Spaghetti

  • Basil

  1. Boil a pot of water and cook your dry spaghetti in it for about eight minutes.

  2. Get a pan searing hot and put a tiny drizzle of olive oil in it.

  3. Cook the meatballs in the pan until a nice crust has formed on all sides.

  4. Once the spagetti is done, drain the noodles and pour warmed tomato sauce over them.

  5. Combine the meatballs with the spaghetti, sprinke some chopped basil on top and enjoy!
