Chocolate Pie

A delicious slice of the chocolate pie we'll be making today!

One of my favoire desserts is the humble chocolate pie. Nothing goes better with a cup of coffee after a big meal than a slice of this heavenly delight! I learned this recipe from my momma and I'm excited to be able to teach it to you today!

It's a super simple recipe which makes it perfect for any occasion, be it a weekday dinner or a supper club? Try this one today, I don't think you'll be disapointed!

You'll need the following ingredients...

  • Sugar

  • Baking Soda

  • Eggs

  • Pie Crust

  • Cocoa Powder

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and cocoa powder together in a bowl.

  2. Pour the mixture into your pie crust.

  3. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees.

  4. Bake pie for 20 minutes. To test if the pie is ready, stick a toothpick into the center of the pie. If, when removed, the toothpick is clean, the pie is ready!

  5. Take the pie out of the oven and let rest for ten minutes. Serve and enjoy!
